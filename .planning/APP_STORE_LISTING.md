## Long Description (App Store listing body)

### Stop copy-pasting tracking numbers one by one.

TrackFlow lets your team upload a single CSV file and instantly bulk-fulfill all matching orders with the correct tracking numbers — in seconds, not hours.

Whether you're fulfilling 10 orders or 10,000, TrackFlow saves hours of manual work every single week.

---

### How it works

1. **Export tracking numbers** from your shipping carrier or 3PL (any CSV format works)
2. **Upload the CSV** — TrackFlow auto-detects your columns (order number, tracking number, carrier)
3. **Review the mapping** — confirm or adjust which column maps to what
4. **Run the import** — TrackFlow bulk-updates Shopify and triggers customer shipping notifications automatically

That's it. No scripts, no API keys, no developer required.

---

### Key Features

**📦 Any CSV format accepted**
No rigid template required. TrackFlow reads your CSV headers and intelligently maps columns — even if you call your column "Order #", "order_id", or "Reference".

**🔍 Automatic carrier detection**
TrackFlow identifies the carrier from the tracking number pattern — no manual entry needed. Supported carriers include:
- UPS
- FedEx
- USPS
- DHL Express
- DHL eCommerce
- OnTrac
- LaserShip
- Canada Post
- Royal Mail (UK)
- Australia Post
- Amazon Logistics

**📧 Automatic customer notifications**
When TrackFlow marks an order as fulfilled, Shopify automatically sends the customer their shipping confirmation email — no extra setup.

**📊 Full import history & audit log**
Every import run is logged with per-row success/failure details. Easily identify which orders couldn't be matched and why.

**💳 Free plan included**
Get started with 50 orders/month at no cost. Upgrade to Pro ($19/month) for unlimited imports.
